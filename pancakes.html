Pancakes

These fluffy pancakes are perfect for a weekend breakfast! They're easy to make and always a hit with kids and adults alike.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 ¼ cups milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • ¼ cup melted butter

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together milk, eggs, and melted butter.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  4. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat.
  5. Pour ¼ cup batter for each pancake onto the griddle.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown on the other side.
